We use the anonymous data we collect solely for the purpose of improving the App for our users. Specifically, we use this information to:

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent any user information to third parties because we do not collect any personal information in the first place. Anonymous, aggregated data may be used for internal analysis but will never be shared in a way that could identify an individual user.
