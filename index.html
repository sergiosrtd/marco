Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App depends on the content and materials you use, and includes:

While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you register for an account.

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, such as the features you use, the recipes you view, searches you conduct, and the time and date of your visits.

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Personalize your app experience by showing you recipes and content we think you might like.
  • Enable user-to-user communications (e.g., comments on recipes).
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the App.
  • Notify you of updates to the App.
  • Respond to your comments and questions and provide customer service.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our App.
